This portal offers an exhaustive archive of all definitive coins issued in India after it became a republic in 1950. Learn about the evolution of everyday coinage through distinct phases — from the Naya Paisa Series and decimalization, to modern stainless steel and bi-metallic issues.

Highlights include:

  • Complete chronology of definitive coin series from 1950 to present
  • Details on metal composition, denominations, and design changes
  • Special series like the Unity in Diversity (2004), Hasta Mudra (2007), and Grain Series (2019)

Discover the stories behind every commemorative coin issued post-1950. These coins celebrate national figures, cultural icons, historic milestones, and government initiatives.

Key features:

  • Coin-by-coin listing with themes, years, and minting details
  • Insight into rare and limited-edition issues

Use the navigation to browse by year, denomination, or coin type. Whether you're looking to catalog your collection or dive into the historical journey of Indian coins, everything starts here.
